Why Grafter?

Size doesn't matter

Grafter uses Clojure lazy sequences so you can work on any volume of data or on streams without worrying about memory.

All data formats

Grafter allows you to work on all tabular data formats including CSV and Excel.

Collaboration and sharing

If you're not an expert or you don't need to work at a low level, it's easy to reuse templates.

High quality Linked Data

Performance, control and separation of concerns assures high quality.

Complete integration (coming soon)

An importer is coming so Grafter will be totally integrated into your workflow.

Robustness to changes in data

If your data evolves over time, the changes will be painless with Grafter.

Easy templating

Describe the RDF you want to create using simple templates that look just like Turtle.

Error reporting

Facilities for developers and pipeline builders easily to incorporate error detation into their transformations.

For all users (coming soon)

Grafter is suitable for both developers - with its programmable DSL - and Excel experts with its (forthcoming) Excel-like interface.
